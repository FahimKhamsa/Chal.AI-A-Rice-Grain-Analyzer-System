Last updated: June 7, 2025. This Privacy Policy explains how Chal.AI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using Chal.AI, you agree to the practices described here.

1 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account Information Email address, encrypted password Authentication and account management
Images Photos of rice grains you capture or upload AI analysis — images are processed and then discarded
Analysis Results Grain type, quality metrics, timestamps Displaying your analysis history
Device Permissions Camera, photo library, notifications Core app functionality — only requested when needed
Usage Data App session data (anonymized) Improving app performance

We do not collect: location data, contacts, browsing history, payment information, or device identifiers beyond what is strictly necessary.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Chal.AI service
  • To perform AI-based rice grain classification on images you submit
  • To authenticate you and manage your account securely
  • To display your analysis history within the app
  • To send optional push notifications about completed analyses
  • To generate exportable PDF reports of your analyses

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party.

3 Data Storage & Third-Party Services

Chal.AI uses the following trusted third-party services to operate:

Service Purpose Data Sent Privacy Policy
Supabase Authentication & database Email, analysis results supabase.com/privacy
RunPod AI inference (image analysis) Rice grain images (transient) runpod.io/legal

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Images sent to the AI inference engine are processed transiently and are not permanently stored by the inference provider.

4 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Only with the providers listed in Section 3, under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Safety: To prevent fraud or protect the rights and safety of users.

5 Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Analysis history: Stored until you delete it or close your account.
  • Images: Not permanently stored after AI processing is complete.
  • Deleted accounts: All associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion.

6 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Export your analysis history as a PDF from within the app.
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke camera or notification permissions at any time in your device Settings.

To exercise any right, contact us at the email address in Section 10.

7 Children's Privacy

Chal.AI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8 Security

  • All data is transmitted using TLS/HTTPS encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text (managed by Supabase Auth).
  • Cleartext HTTP traffic is disabled in the app (android:usesCleartextTraffic="false").
  • We regularly review our security practices.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.
